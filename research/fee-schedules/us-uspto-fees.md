## 2. Trademark Fees — eff. 2025-01-18

Sources: [Trademark fee information](https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/trademark-fee-information) · [Table of Trademark Fees XLSX](https://www.uspto.gov/sites/default/files/documents/Trademark-Fees-Current-Final-Unit-Cost-FR-2025.xlsx) · [Examination Guide 1-25](https://www.uspto.gov/sites/default/files/documents/TM-ExamGuide-1-25.pdf) · [Trademark Fee Changes Summary](https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/fees-payment-information/summary-2025-trademark-fee-changes)

**Application fees — STRUCTURE CHANGED.** The TEAS Plus / TEAS Standard split was abolished on 2025-01-18. Sections 1/44 applications now use a single base filing fee per class with surcharges for incompleteness. Section 66(a) (Madrid) retains a separate fee.

| Fee | Amount (per class, electronic) |
|---|---:|
| Base application — §1 or §44 (Trademark Center) | **$350** |
| Surcharge — insufficient information (per class) | $100 |
| Surcharge — using free-text identification not in ID Manual | $200 |
| Surcharge — each additional group of 1,000 characters in free-text ID | $200 |
| Application — §66(a) Madrid request for extension of protection | $600 |
| Intent-to-use: Amendment to Allege Use (§1(c)) | $150 |
| Intent-to-use: Statement of Use (§1(d)) | $150 |
| §8 Declaration of Use (years 5–6) | $325 |
| §9 Renewal application (every 10 years) | $325 |
| §8 / §9 grace-period surcharge | $100 |
| §15 Declaration of Incontestability (optional) | $250 |

Trademark filings have been [mandatory-electronic since 2020-02-15](https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/laws/rule-making-trademark-federal-register-notices-and-comments); paper-filing fees still appear on the schedule but are functionally unavailable.

## 3. Notes

- **Major restructuring in Jan 2025.** First broad patent fee rule since 2020 ([89 FR 91898](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2024/11/20/2024-26821/setting-and-adjusting-patent-fees-during-fiscal-year-2025)) and first major trademark fee restructuring in years ([89 FR 91062](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2024/11/18/2024-26644/setting-and-adjusting-trademark-fees-during-fiscal-year-2025)). Patent fees rose ~7.5% on average plus targeted increases; trademark side replaced TEAS Plus/Standard with a single base + surcharge model and introduced new continuing-application fees (CAFs) on continuations/divisionals filed 6+/9+ years after earliest benefit date.
- **PDF "Last Revised 2026-05-01"** is a republish of the same Jan-2025 fee figures, not a new rule — the [USPTO fee schedule landing page](https://www.uspto.gov/learning-and-resources/fees-and-payment/uspto-fee-schedule) still cites the January 19, 2025 effective date.
- **Director's §10 fee-setting authority sunsets 2026-09-16** per the AIA — any further FY26 adjustment must clear rulemaking before then or revert to statutory default schedule under 35 U.S.C. §41 + cost-recovery for §10-set fees. No FY26 fee NPRM has been published as of this snapshot.
- **Discount levels** were enlarged by the [Unleashing American Innovators Act of 2022](https://www.uspto.gov/learning-and-resources/fees-and-payment/summary-2025-patent-fee-changes) (small 50% → 60%, micro 75% → 80%), effective Dec. 29, 2022; the Jan 2025 schedule is the first post-UAIA full reset.
