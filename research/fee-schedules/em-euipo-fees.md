# EUIPO Fee Schedules — EUTM and EU Design (REUD)

**Snapshot date:** 2026-05-16
**Jurisdiction:** European Union (EUIPO, Alicante)
**Currency:** EUR

---

## 1. EU Trade Mark (EUTM) fees

**Primary source:** [Fees payable direct to EUIPO — Trade marks](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/trade-marks/before-applying/fees-payable-direct-to-the-euipo)
**Fee calculator / overview:** [Fees and payments — Trade marks](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/trade-marks/before-applying/fees-payments)
**Legal basis:** [EU Trade Mark Regulation — Fees (Annex I, EUTMR (EU) 2017/1001)](https://euipo.europa.eu/ohimportal/en/eu-trade-mark-regulation-fees)

### Application — individual EUTM

| Item | Online (e-filing) | Paper |
|---|---|---|
| Basic fee, 1 class | **EUR 850** | **EUR 1,000** |
| Second class | EUR 50 | EUR 50 |
| Each class from the third onward | EUR 150 | EUR 150 |

Paper filing carries a **EUR 150 surcharge** on the basic fee versus e-filing. Practice note: EUIPO strongly steers filers to e-filing — paper is now rare.

### Opposition

- Opposition fee: **EUR 320**
- Filed within 3 months of publication of the EUTM application.
- Source: [Opposition — EUIPO](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/trade-marks/after-applying/opposition)

### Renewal (every 10 years, indefinitely renewable)

| Item | Online | Paper |
|---|---|---|
| Renewal, 1 class | **EUR 850** | EUR 1,000 |
| Second class | EUR 50 | EUR 50 |
| Each class from the third onward | EUR 150 | EUR 150 |

Renewal fees mirror filing fees, per EUTMR Annex I.

## 2. EU Design fees (REUD — formerly RCD)

**Primary source:** [Fees payable direct to EUIPO — Designs](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/designs/before-applying/fees-payable-direct-to-the-euipo)
**Overview:** [Fees and payments — Designs](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/designs/before-applying/fees-payments)
**EUIPO Guidelines, §7.5.2:** [Fees payable for RCDs / REUDs](https://guidelines.euipo.europa.eu/2213908/1948937/designs-guidelines/7-5-2-fees-payable-for-rcds)
**Legal basis:** Council Regulation (EC) No 6/2002 as amended by [Regulation (EU) 2024/2822](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/2822/oj) (the "Design Amending Regulation"), Phase I in force 1 May 2025.

### Application

| Item | Fee |
|---|---|
| **Application fee, first design** (now bundles registration + publication) | **EUR 350** |
| Each additional design in a multiple application | **EUR 125** |
| Deferment of publication (replaces publication fee if requested) | **EUR 40** (per design) |

Notes:
- The pre-reform structure split registration (EUR 230) + publication (EUR 120) = EUR 350. The new single **EUR 350 application fee** is numerically equivalent for design 1 but the multiple-design discount band was eliminated (the old per-design "additional" fee tier of EUR 175 / EUR 80 / EUR 50 is now a flat EUR 125 per additional design).
- Late-payment surcharges for registration / deferment have been **abolished**.

### Renewal (every 5 years, max 25 years total)

A REUD lasts 5 years from filing and is renewable in 4 successive 5-year periods.

| Renewal period | Years covered | Fee per design |
|---|---|---|
| 1st renewal | 5 → 10 | **EUR 150** (was EUR 90) |
| 2nd renewal | 10 → 15 | **EUR 250** (was EUR 120) |
| 3rd renewal | 15 → 20 | **EUR 400** (was EUR 150) |
| 4th renewal | 20 → 25 | **EUR 700** (was EUR 180) |

There is no "renewal at year 25" — protection terminates after the 4th renewal. The user's prompt listed 5/10/15/20/25; the correct schedule has **4 renewal events** at years 5, 10, 15, and 20.

## 4. Notes — recent changes

- **Major design fee restructure confirmed.** Reg. (EU) 2024/2822 entered into force 1 May 2025. Highlights:
  - Terminology change: **Registered Community Design (RCD) → Registered European Union Design (REUD)**. The "RCD" label is still in heavy circulation; treat the two as synonymous for now.
  - Registration + publication fees merged into a single **EUR 350 application fee**.
  - Renewal fees increased sharply, especially at later periods (4th renewal nearly **4x** prior level: EUR 180 → EUR 700).
  - Late-payment surcharges for registration/deferment abolished.
  - Renewal **deadline** rule changed: basic period now ends on the exact expiry date, not the last day of that month.
  - Source: [Design renewals following the EU Design Reform — EUIPO News](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/news/design-renewals-following-the-eu-design-reform-what-to-know); [2025 Designs Reform edition of the Guidelines](https://www.euipo.europa.eu/en/news/the-2025-euipo-guidelines).
- **Hague alignment:** the user's framing is partially correct. The reform aligns EU designs more closely with Hague terminology and procedure (e.g. dropping the "community" label, unbundling fees), but the fee *amounts* themselves were not pinned to Hague tariffs — they were set independently in Annex I to the amended Regulation.
- **EUTM fees:** **no recent change.** The EUTMR Annex I figures (EUR 850 / 50 / 150 / 320 / renewal mirror) have been stable since the 2017 EUTMR took effect.
