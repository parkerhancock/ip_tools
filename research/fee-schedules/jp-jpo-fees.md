## 1. Patents

Source row: ["1. Application — (1)Patents"](https://www.jpo.go.jp/e/system/process/tesuryo/hyou.html)
and ["3. Annual fee / Registration fee — (1)Patents"](https://www.jpo.go.jp/e/system/process/tesuryo/hyou.html).

### 1.1 Filing

| Action | Fee (¥) |
|---|---|
| Patent application | **14,000** |
| Application in foreign language | 22,000 |
| Description+drawings filed by reference to an earlier application | 14,000 |
| National phase entry (PCT) | 14,000 |
| Application for registration of extension of term | 74,000 |

### 1.2 Request for examination (filings on/after 1 Apr 2019)

The examination request fee is a **base fee + per-claim fee**. The base
and per-claim rate both depend on whether (and by whom) an ISR has been
established:

| Path | Base (¥) | Per-claim (¥) | Formula |
|---|---|---|---|
| Direct national filing / no ISR available | **138,000** | **4,000** | `138,000 + 4,000 × N` |
| ISR by JPO (PCT) | 83,000 | 2,400 | `83,000 + 2,400 × N` |
| ISR by an ISA other than JPO (PCT) | 124,000 | 3,600 | `124,000 + 3,600 × N` |
| Report by a designated searching organization | 110,000 | 3,200 | `110,000 + 3,200 × N` |

where `N` = number of claims at the time the examination request is
filed. (Older formula applies to pre-1 Apr 2019 filings: base ¥118,000 +
¥4,000/claim.)

### 1.3 Patent fees / annuities (applications filed on/after 1 Jan 1988
and examination requested on/after 1 Apr 2004 — i.e. the schedule that
governs effectively all live patents today)

| Year band | Annual base (¥) | Per-claim add-on (¥) |
|---|---|---|
| 1st – 3rd year | **4,300** | 300 |
| 4th – 6th year | **10,300** | 800 |
| 7th – 9th year | **24,800** | 1,900 |
| 10th – 25th year | **59,400** | 4,600 |

> Years 1–3 are paid as a single lump sum at grant (they double as the
> registration fee). Year 4 onwards is paid annually by the patent
> anniversary; non-residents pay direct per the
> [Patent annual fee payment for non-residents](https://www.jpo.go.jp/e/system/process/tesuryo/160401_renewing_outside.html)
> page.

## Notes — recent / scheduled changes

- The currently-effective schedule is the **1 April 2022** revision.
  That revision **raised the patent examination request base fee from
  ¥118,000 to ¥138,000 (+17%)** and raised most patent annuity rates;
  trademark registration / renewal per-class fees rose materially as
  well (the prior renewal was ¥38,800/class; now ¥43,600/class). See the
  practitioner write-up from
  [ONDA TECHNO, "JPO Fee Increases Effective April 1, 2022"](https://www.ondatechno.com/en/news/patent/p1361/)
  and [Lexology summary](https://www.lexology.com/library/detail.aspx?g=5bca7ce8-a27e-495f-8c3d-d63f0e3750a9)
  for context; the binding numbers above are pulled from the JPO page
  itself.
- The JPO Design Act revision effective **1 April 2020** extended the
  term of design rights from 20 years to 25 years and replaced the
  multiple-design surcharge with the flat ¥16,000 per-design fee shown
  above.
- The JPO operates a **half-refund system** on examination request fees
  in some early-withdrawal scenarios — see
  [Half Refund System on Examination Request Fees](https://www.jpo.go.jp/e/system/process/tesuryo/henkan.html).
- Fee reductions apply for SMEs, micro-entities, universities and other
  qualifying applicants (substantial — up to two-thirds off examination
  and annuity fees in the 1st–10th years).
- No further headline JPO fee increase has been announced as of the
  snapshot date (no notice on the
  [Fee Information landing page](https://www.jpo.go.jp/e/system/process/tesuryo/index.html)).
